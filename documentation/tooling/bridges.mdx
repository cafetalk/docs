# Bridges

Bridge assets between Ethereum and T-Rex Chain using the Arbitrum Bridge.

## Arbitrum Bridge

The official bridge for T-Rex Chain is integrated with the Arbitrum Bridge infrastructure.

### Bridge URL

**Mainnet Bridge**: [https://bridge.arbitrum.io/?destinationChain=t-rex&sourceChain=ethereum](https://bridge.arbitrum.io/?destinationChain=t-rex&sourceChain=ethereum)

## How to Bridge

<Steps>
  <Step title="Connect Wallet">
    Connect your wallet (MetaMask, WalletConnect, etc.) to the bridge interface
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Select Networks">
    Choose source chain (Ethereum) and destination chain (T-Rex)
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Select Asset">
    Choose the token you want to bridge
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Enter Amount">
    Enter the amount you want to bridge
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Review and Confirm">
    Review the transaction details and confirm
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Wait for Confirmation">
    Wait for confirmations on both chains (typically 5-10 minutes)
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Troubleshooting

### Bridge Stuck

If your bridge transaction appears stuck:

1. Check the transaction status on both explorers
2. Wait for additional confirmations
3. Contact support if the issue persists

### Failed Bridge

If a bridge transaction fails:

1. Check you have sufficient gas on the source chain
2. Verify the bridge supports the asset you're bridging
3. Ensure you have sufficient balance

<Tip>
For testnet, use the testnet explorer to find test tokens. The mainnet bridge is for production use only.

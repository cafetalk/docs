# Bridge

Cross-chain bridge services enable seamless asset transfers between T-Rex Chain and other blockchain networks.

## Official Bridge

### Arbitrum Bridge

The official bridge for T-Rex Chain is integrated with the Arbitrum Bridge infrastructure.

**Bridge URL**: [https://bridge.arbitrum.io/?destinationChain=t-rex&sourceChain=ethereum](https://bridge.arbitrum.io/?destinationChain=t-rex&sourceChain=ethereum)

## How Bridge Works

<Steps>
  <Step title="Initiate Transfer">
    Select source chain, destination chain, and asset amount
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Lock Assets">
    Assets are locked on the source chain
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Verification">
    Bridge verifies the transaction on the source chain
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Mint on Destination">
    Equivalent assets are minted on T-Rex Chain
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Receive Assets">
    Assets appear in your wallet on T-Rex Chain
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Bridge Time

- **Estimated Time**: 5-10 minutes for standard bridges
- **Confirmation Blocks**: Requires confirmations on both chains
- **Status Tracking**: Track bridge status on the explorer

## Bridge Fees

- **Bridge Fee**: Minimal fee for the bridge service
- **Gas Fees**: Standard gas fees apply on both chains
- **Total Cost**: Displayed before confirmation

## Using the Bridge

1. Visit the [Arbitrum Bridge](https://bridge.arbitrum.io/?destinationChain=t-rex&sourceChain=ethereum)
2. Connect your wallet
3. Select source and destination chains
4. Choose the asset and amount
5. Review and confirm the transaction
6. Wait for confirmations on both chains

<Warning>
Always verify you're using the official bridge URL. Double-check the domain before connecting your wallet.
</Warning>

<Tip>
For testnet, use the testnet explorer to find test tokens. The mainnet bridge is for production use only.
</Tip>

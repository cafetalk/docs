## Overview

T-Rex is committed to building an open and thriving blockchain ecosystem that supports various types of applications and projects.

## Open Ecosystem

The T-Rex ecosystem is designed to be open and interoperable, with a focus on empowering DApp developers and projects.

### Smart Contracts

All relevant contracts are public and verified, allowing for full transparency and audibility:

- **T-Rex Passport**: Universal on-chain DID
- **T-Rex Badge**: Badge validation and minting system

### Open SDKs

Comprehensive open SDKs to facilitate rapid integration:

- **T-Rex Passport SDK**: Integrate T-Rex Passport into applications
- **T-Rex Badge SDK**: Create custom badge systems
- **Matching Engine API**: Access the Economic Symbiosis Engine
